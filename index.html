Star Identification Algorithms

The Cal Poly CubeSat lab has been developing a low-cost
startracker to fly on cubesat missions. An integral
component of a startracker is the star identification
algorithm. This algorithm is used to map stars in a photo
to stars in a catalog, which provides enough information
to determine a satellite's attitude. My senior project
revolved around studying and comparing the algorithms used to solve
this problem.
